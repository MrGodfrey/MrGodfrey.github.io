Should students take smart phones to school

2018-06-03 | 随笔

Preparations for a debate in an oral English class. Writing in English and Chinese.

Argument

Students should take smart phone to school.

Smart phones are good for education

First, you can use smart phones for searching information. The science and technology change so rapidly. In addition to books, the internet is a more convenient and quick way to obtain the latest knowledge.

Next, there are already many excellent educational apps on smart phones. You can learn programming, do virtual experments, talk to people in other countries.

Last, some convenient features such as calculators, dctionaries, translations, can save students a lot of time. And most importantly, you can do it all with just one smartphone.

Smart phones can allow students to prepare for modern life.

We must not only teach students how to study, but also teach students how to live. Students should have early access to new technologies such as smart phones, so they can build more modern ideas and opinions.

If you tell students in the class room the benefits of science and technology, while strictly prohibiting technology products such as smart phones from entering the school. Isn't it ironic?

It is better to guide students how to use smart phones corrrectly and how to distinguish interesting positive information from impetuous speech, rather than blindly prohibiting smart phones.

some preparation

There is bad content in Internet

Today's search engines and all major websites also have advanved grading technology that can protect students from bad content on the Internet.

Smart phones will cause comparisons among classmates

All things can lead to comparisons, clothes, shoes, and even hairstyles. You can't prohibit them all.

On the other hand, comparisons are human nature, students should learn how to restrain their comparisons, rather than avoid comparisons.

Smart phones can cause radiation

Staying up late can cause greater health damage, but there are so many students have to stay up late.

And high-voltage lines have much stronger radiation.

中文

手机有利于学习。

智能手机可以让学生方便的搜索资料，科学技术的发展日新月异，除了书本以外，网络是更方便快捷的获取最新知识的渠道。

手机上已经有很多成熟的教育App，学生可以学习编程，做虚拟实验,与其他国家的人交谈等等。

手机上诸如计算器，词典，翻译，等等方便的功能可以节省学生大量的时间。

手机可以让学生为现代生活做好准备

我们不仅要教会学生如何学习，更要教会学生如何去生活。学生应当更早的接触智能设备等等新技术，才能够更早的建立起更为现代的思想和观念。

如果课堂上告诉学生科学技术的各种好处，同时又严令禁止科技产品进入校园，这不是有些讽刺吗？

与其一味禁止手机，不如引导学生如何正确使用手机，如何分辨有趣的正面的资讯和浮躁虚无的言论。

一些准备

网上有不健康的内容

现在的搜索引擎以及各个主要网站也有先进的分级技术，可以保障学生不会在网络上浏览到不好的内容。

手机会造成同学的攀比

所有的东西都可以导致攀比，衣服，鞋子，文具甚至是发型。不能借此就禁止手机。

另一方面，攀比是人性，学生也应当学会如何克制自己的攀比心理，而不是一味的避免攀比。

手机会造成辐射

熬夜对身体造成的伤害更大。

而且高压线有着强得多的辐射.
