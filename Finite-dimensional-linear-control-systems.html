Finite-dimensional linear control systems

2018-11-13 | 2018-11-16 | 数学 ， 学习笔记

In this chapter, we review some of standard facts on the controllability of linear finite-dimensional linear control systems.

This chapter is organized as follows.

In section 1 (ODE and Control Systems), we begin with some basic facts on the solvable theory of ordinary differential equations(ODE for short). We set up notation and terminology of control theory. At the end of the section, we will restrict our attention to linear systems.

In section 2 (The criterion for controllability), we are going to give the necessary and sufficient condition for the controllability of the linear control system. We will prove that the controllability is equivalent to the reversibility of the controllability Gramian. This equivalence is called an integral criterion for controllability. We will also give the famous Kalman rank condition.
