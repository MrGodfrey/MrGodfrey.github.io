person Instructor: Yu Wang

Course Description

This intensive graduate-level course provides a comprehensive introduction to the fundamentals of neural networks and the cutting-edge developments in deep learning. Students will explore the mathematical foundations, architectural innovations, and practical implementation strategies that drive modern AI.

Optimization Computer Vision NLP Transformers

Main Topics

  • 01

    Linear Algebra & Calculus Review

    The mathematical bedrock of gradient descent and backpropagation.

  • 02

    Convolutional Neural Networks

    Spatial hierarchies and feature extraction for visual tasks.

  • 03

    Sequence Modeling & Attention

    RNNs, LSTMs, and the rise of the Transformer architecture.

  • 04

    Generative Models

    VAEs, GANs, and Diffusion models in modern AI.
